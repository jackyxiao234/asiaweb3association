About Us

Pioneering Web3 Adoption Across Asia

Founded in 2018, Asia Web3 Association has emerged as the premier investment bank bridging traditional finance with decentralized technologies. Our team of blockchain experts, financial veterans, and regulatory specialists work at the intersection of innovation and institutional-grade investment strategies.

With headquarters in Singapore and offices across Hong Kong, Tokyo, and Seoul, we've facilitated over $2.8B in Web3 investments, connecting visionary founders with strategic capital across the Asia-Pacific region.

Our unique value proposition combines deep technical understanding of blockchain architectures with traditional investment banking expertise, enabling us to identify and nurture the most promising Web3 projects at every stage of growth.
